Rashgulla

Ingridents

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, then add lemon juice to curdle it.
  2. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve.
  3. Rinse the curdled milk (chenna) with cold water to remove acidity
  4. Squeeze out excess water and hang for 1-2 hours to drain further.
  5. Mash the chenna into a smooth paste.
  6. Shape into small balls and keep aside.
  7. Add the rasgulla balls to the syrup and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and let cool. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Barfi

Ingridents

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • ghee

Recipe

  1. Combine milk powder, sugar, and cardamom powder in a pan.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of ghee and mix well.
  3. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and forms a mass (about 15-20 minutes).
  4. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  5. Shape into desired form (e.g., square, rectangle, or round).
